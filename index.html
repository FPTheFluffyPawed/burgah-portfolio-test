Through either self publishing or collaboration with other artists, I have contributed work towards multiple comic or magazine projects, meeting set deadlines and providing comics or designs fulfilling the requirements set by organisers. The included works consist of pages from "Psychic Wars", a short comic in the ConfliX independant Zine I have published, and Concept work for "The Hand Of God", an ongoing project I am collaborating on with a seperate writer.
Frequently, I will take commissions briefs from individuals, seeking either a personal illustrated piece or a character design. These briefs will often involve frequent communication with the client, keeping them updated on progress whilst also ensuring that work is delivered as quickly as possible whilst also meeting all the clients requirements.
Work for a collaborative ttrpg project. For this brief I was required to produce vehicle designs that would fit into an already established setting whilst also reflecting the roles that the vehicles would fill gameplay wise, the personality of the faction using the vehicles and being simple enough shape wise to be converted into basic 3d models.
Inbetween work for clients I will frequently produce personal work, with the primary focuses being those of character design. These will then branch into more elaborate personal projects, be they world building or writing exercises or vehicle and biology exploration.
